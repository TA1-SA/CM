


===================
Failover Clustering 
===================
 are groups of computers that support server applications that can be reliably utilized with a minimum amount of down-time.


*Prerequisites:
1-domain 
2-at least two servers joined to domain with same version and edition  
3-shared storage 

*LAB steps :
1-create two servers with same version and edition ( node1-mhmad  node2-mhmad enterpriseStorage-mhmad)
2-join the servers to domain
3-enable failover clustering feature on the two servers
4-configure the failover cluster on the main server
